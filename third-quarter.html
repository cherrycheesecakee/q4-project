Laufey

“As a musician, my goal is to bring jazz and classical music to my generation,” - Laufey

Laufey (lāy-vāy) was born April 23 1999, and is an Icelandic singer-songwriter, whose based in Los Angeles. Her popularity rose during early 2020 as a jazz-inspired pop artist, which was surprising for some as jazz is a dying genre.

Her most popular album is, Bewitched, and her popular songs are - From The Start, Falling Behind, Valentine, Let You Break My Heart Again, and Promise.

She preformed as a cello soloist in the Iceland Symphony Orchesyta at 15 years old, and was a finalist in Iceland's Got Talent at 2014 and a semi-finalist on The Voice Iceland during 2015. She released her debut EP, Typical of Me(2021), and debut album, Everything I Know About Love(2022). Her album, Bewitched(2023) won the best traditional pop vocal album during the Grammy Awards(2024).

She has a simple and casual fasion style, and she mixes elements like lace, pastel colors, and bright metallic elements. People describe her fasion style as an "extension to her music".
